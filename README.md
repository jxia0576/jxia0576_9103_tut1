Inspiration: Doctor Strange's Magic Circle Visual Effects

I drew inspiration from Doctor Strange's magic circle visual effects—where intricate geometric lines intertwine with luminous patterns to evoke a potent fusion of technological sophistication and mysticism. I sought to borrow this visual language of symbolic geometry and energy flow for my project, aiming to represent the latent forces within information systems. This approach not only possesses aesthetic appeal but also symbolises the concept of “control and order”.
